01 Brand Introduction

LIONNA is a Brazilian luxury active lifestyle brand launching its first capsule collection for Spring/Summer 2027. Our category is Luxury Active Living — premium women's activewear designed for the sophisticated woman who moves through her entire day in one elegant wardrobe: from pilates studio to boardroom, from tennis court to dinner table.

We are seeking OEM manufacturing partners for our Phase 1 launch. We require private label production with custom branding applied, premium fabric standards, and full quality compliance before bulk production. All garments will carry the LIONNA™ mark exclusively — no supplier branding on any finished piece.
02 Brand & Quality Standards
Leggings, bras, shorts, tanks: 78% Polyamide (Nylon) + 22% Elastane
Wide/Straight-leg pants: 95% Polyester + 5% Spandex (woven tech, fluid drape)
Bomber jacket: Scuba or Ponte knit (70% Poly + 25% Nylon + 5% Spandex)
Windbreaker/layer jacket: Nylon/Spandex preferred
Jumpsuit: Premium nylon/spandex · matte finish · no shine
GSM leggings/shorts: 250–280 g/m² minimum
· Zero transparency: squat + flash + back-light on lightest color
· Colorfastness: ISO 105-C06 Grade 4–5 minimum
· Pilling: ISO 12945-2 Grade 4 min after 2,000 cycles
· Stretch recovery: ASTM D2594 ≥95% after 50% stretch
· Seam integrity: 500-cycle stress test inner thigh + glute
· Waistband: must not roll during 30-min movement test
· Safety: Oeko-Tex Standard 100 or REACH required
· Woven label: Onyx base + metallic Sand Gold thread · 55×22mm · all styles
· Chest embroidery: LIONNA symbol 20mm · left chest · tone-on-tone or Gold
· Hang tag: 400gsm two-sided · Gold hot stamp · Stone cord · brass eyelet
· Jacquard waistband: 50mm · symbol repeat 80mm · Gold thread · leggings/shorts/pants
· Gold zipper YKK Excella (or premium equiv): bomber + jumpsuit only
· Polybag: frosted matte LDPE · symbol + LIONNA · Gold 1-color print
· Basic polyester in leggings or bras
· Cotton or French Terry for any core piece
· Any logo or branding other than LIONNA on finished garment
· Sewn-in bojo/pad — must be removable via pocket
· "Butt lift", "scrunch", "push-up" silhouettes
· Hoodies with large hoods (not LIONNA aesthetic)
· Color-block or printed patterns (solid colors only)
